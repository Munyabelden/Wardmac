Transportation

Nuclear, Radioactive and Medical Equipment

When it comes to the transportation of radioactive materials, utmost caution is imperative. These materials, classified as nuclear elements, possess unstable nuclei, thereby emitting excess energy in the form of radiation. This inherent instability underscores the critical need for meticulous handling and robust safety measures throughout the transportation process. We specialize in the safe transport of nuclear and medical equipment, leveraging our expertise and experience. Serving esteemed clients such as Eskom, Curtis Wright, and NTP, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of safety and reliability
